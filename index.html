THE ACADEMY'S MISSION AND VISSION

MISSION STATEMENT

Tumana Good Seed Christian Academy Inc. express three-fold task.

1. To provide students with relevant Christian education by:

  • Introducing teaching of the Lord Jesus Christ found in the Holy Bible.
  • Teaching and exemplyfying the love of God in words and in deeds.
  • Developing their physical, intellectual, moral and social potentials.

2. To facilitate the intellectual development of the students by:

  • Molding the attitude and strengthening the love for truth, peace and equality;
  • Teaching the basic and functioning knowledge which would be applied to and relevant to the practically of life; and
  • Providing Significant and realistisc situations with opportunity of learning by doing

3. To develop creative expression and aesthetic value of the individual by:

  • Teaching how to appreciate God's creation; and
  • Giving opportunity to express his individuality as a person.

‎

VISION STATEMENT

TUMANA GOOD SEED CHRISTIAN ACADEMY INC., shall provide an education, which would lead each individual to develop spiritual, emotional, intellectual and social being.

TUMANA GOOD SEED CHRISTIAN ACADEMY INC., shall teach individual with utmost desire to inculcate the love of God, to integrate Christian vaues necessary to everyday life and to contribute toward a goal of a Christ-like Personality.

TUMANA GOOD SEED CHRISTIAN ACADEMY INC., shall undertake the introduction of Filipino ideas and shall commit to the development of the Filipino learnners in acquiring the basic skills essential in preparing himself in higher levels of learning and in facing the challenges of the over changing society where he/she belongs.

TUMANA GOOD SEED CHRISTIAN ACADEMY INC., shall devote himself to imbue deep love for his own country, language, and culture. He/she also carry the task of molding Filipino, ready to share his/her talents, capacity, training, and his/her learning in the service of national development.
